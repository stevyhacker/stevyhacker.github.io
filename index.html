About

I am a Computer Science student and self-taught mobile developer from Niksic, Montenegro. I'm a huge online education enthusiast and at the moment I am learning web developmet at freecodecamp. I regularly enroll new online courses and read a lot about latest trends and technologies in the world of app development.

I have developed a dozen of android applications. You can see some of them at my Play Store developer account. as well as in my portfolio here.
I do native applications with Java and I have worked with REST APIs, SQLite databases, Realm, Google Maps APIs, Admob, etc. Also I support open-source initiative and I have released source code of most of my applications and games at my github account.
