About

I am a seasoned Software Engineer based in Podgorica, Montenegro (CET), specializing in blockchain and web3 tech.

With over 8 years of diverse experience in the software development industry, I have honed my skills through working in various team settings, as an independent freelancer, and in both on-site and remote capacities. My portfolio showcases a selection of personal projects, reflecting my passion for exploring cutting-edge tech stacks.

I often experiment with side projects, utilizing emerging and personally intriguing tech stacks. I invite you to explore a selection of these initiatives displayed in my portfolio.

In recent years, I have dedicated myself to the blockchain and web3 space, which prompted my transition in 2021 to a full-time Solidity smart contract engineer. I possess expertise in Ethereum (EVM) and employ widely-used tools like Hardhat for the development of decentralized applications.

Having transitioned from a successful career in mobile app development, I have been an avid follower of cryptocurrency development and the Ethereum ecosystem since 2017. My profound interest in DeFi and NFTs led me to fully commit to the role of a smart contract engineer.

My skillset includes more than two years of experience with Solidity, utilizing Hardhat, Foundry, ethers.js, and frontend development expertise in React/Next.js and TypeScript. Throughout my web2 career, I primarily focused on Android mobile app development using Kotlin and Java, acquiring solid software engineering practices in the process.

As a lifelong learner, I have participated in numerous hackathons, workshops, and contests, constantly striving to enhance my technical capabilities and expand my knowledge base. I have also mentored and instructed Android development courses for beginners, while managing and leading a mobile development team.
