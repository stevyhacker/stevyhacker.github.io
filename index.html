About

I am a Software Engineer based in Podgorica, Montenegro (CET).

I have over 6 years of professional software development experience, working in teams, as a solo freelancer, in office or remotely.

I often do side projects with tech stacks that are interesting to me at the moment, and you can check out some of my personal projects in my portfolio here.

Last few years I have been investing a lot of my time in the blockchain/web3 space. That's why I decided in 2021 to switch completely to web3 and work full time as a Solidity smart contract engineer. I work with Ethereum (EVM) and popular tools like Hardhat for building decentralized applications.

I moved on from my mobile app development career to web3. I have been following crypto development and especially the Ethereum ecosystem since 2017. DeFi and NFTs got me very interested, and I decided to go all-in as a smart contract engineer.

I have a year of experience with Solidity, using Hardhat, ethers.js, and also have some frontend dev experience, mostly React/Typescript.

For most of my web2 career, I have been working on Android mobile app development with Kotlin/Java where I learned good software engineering practices. I participated in many hackathons, workshops, contests, and I am always striving to improve my technical skills and broaden my knowledge. I mentored and taught Android development in two beginner courses and have led, and managed a mobile development team as well.
