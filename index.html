About

I am a freelance android developer and Computer Science student from Niksic, Montenegro. Occasionally I also do web development and I am familiar with HTML5, Javascript, SASS, React, etc. Also I've had some experience with PHP and Laravel as well, especially building APIs with Dingo mostly for use in my apps.

I have developed more than a dozen of android applications.
You can see some of them at my personal Play Store developer account. as well as in my portfolio here.

I do native development with Java and I have worked with REST APIs, SQLite databases, Realm, Google Maps APIs, GCM and Firebase, Admob and countless other 3rd party libraries. As a supporter of open source initiative, I have released source code of some of my personal projects at my github account.
