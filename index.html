About

I am a seasoned Software Engineer based in Podgorica, Montenegro (CET), specializing in smart contracts with over 8 years of diverse experience in the software development industry.

I have led and managed a team of mobile developers, while also mentoring and instructing Android development courses for beginners. My interests in DeFi and NFTs led me to switch careers from mobile to blockchain.

My skillset includes more than two years of experience with Solidity, EVM, Hardhat, Foundry, and frontend development expertise in React/Next.js and TypeScript. Throughout my mobile dev career, I primarily focused on native Android app development using Kotlin and Java.
