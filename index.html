About

I am a seasoned Software Engineer based in Podgorica, Montenegro (CET), specializing in smart contracts with over 8 years of diverse experience in the software development industry..

I have led and managed a team of mobile developers, while also mentoring and instructing Android development courses for beginners. Often I experiment with side projects, utilizing emerging and personally intriguing tech stacks. You can explore some of these projects down below.

Having transitioned from a successful career in mobile app development, I have been an avid follower of cryptocurrency development and the Ethereum ecosystem since 2017. My profound interest in DeFi and NFTs led me to fully commit to the role of a smart contract engineer in 2021.

My skillset includes more than two years of experience with Solidity, utilizing Hardhat, Foundry, ethers.js, and frontend development expertise in React/Next.js and TypeScript. Throughout my mobile dev career, I primarily focused on native Android app development using Kotlin and Java, acquiring solid software engineering practices in the process.
