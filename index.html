About

I am a Software Engineer from Niksic, Montenegro. Timezone: CET

I have over 5 years of professional software development experience, mostly in android app development, working in teams, as a solo freelancer, in office or remotely.
You can check out some of my apps at my personal Play Store developer account. as well as in my portfolio here.

I do native development with Kotlin/Java and I have worked with REST APIs(Retrofit), SQLite databases(Room), Realm, Google Maps, GCM. Chromecast, Firebase, RxJava, Coroutines, Dagger, Admob, Exoplayer, Leanback and countless other 3rd party libraries. Some of my older personal projects are available on my GitHub account. Through 2017/18 I had worked a lot on Android TV and Amazon Fire TV platforms as well.

I used to write a blog(mostly about tech) but haven't been very active recently. You can check it out here.
I am also proud of my StackOverflow profile, I use it daily in my work and try to help others when I can.

Last few years I have been investing a lot of my time in the blockchain/web3 space. That's why I decided in 2021 to switch completely to web3 and work full time as a Solidity smart contract engineer. I work with Ethereum (EVM) and popular tools like Hardhat and Ethers for building decentralized applications.
